About ALL INDIA Sports Fest Chakravyuh 2K19

Chakravyuh 2K19 is an annual national level sports fest of IMS Engineering College,Ghaziabad.

This year in this fifteenth edition, we are delighted to share the experience of last year's Chakravyuh, being held for the very first time ever; Chakravyuh was a huge success.

With total participation of more than 1,700 prominent colleges from all over India had come.

Chakravyuh provided a competitive platform to test the resilience of the sportsmen in the most positive way.

The fest is conducted with upmost friendly and euphoric sprite by the college and its students.

Football, Cricket, Basketball, Volleyball, Athletics, Badminton and verity of sport events were conducted.

Chakravyuh is an initiative by the students of IMS Engineering college to inculcate sportsmanship and endurance in students, an attitude to conquer your goal and be the best.

Chakravyuh and its team guarantees you an experience of a lifetime.
