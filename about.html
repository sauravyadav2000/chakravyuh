About IMS Ghaziabad

TITANIUM Jural Fest is an annual national level sports fest of GLS Law College.

This year in the second edition, we are delighted to share the experience of last year's Titanium, being held for the very first time ever; titanium was a huge success.

With total participation of more than 1,700 prominent colleges from all over India had come.

Titanium provided a competitive platform to test the resilience of the sportsmen in the most positive way.

The fest is conducted with upmost friendly and euphoric sprite by the college and its students.

Football, Cricket, Basketball, Volleyball, Athletics, Badminton and verity of sport events were conducted.

Titanium is an initiative by the students of GLS LAW to inculcate sportsmanship and endurance in students, an attitude to conquer your goal and be the No.1

Titanium and its team guarantees you an experience of a lifetime.
